Notes on this redistribution

- Only a sampled subset of the upstream TensorTrust `extraction` and
  `hijacking` attack corpora is bundled in the Koverage repository
  (70 of each, 140 of ~560,000 attacks). The full corpus remains
  available at the upstream HuggingFace repo.
- Context-overflow attacks (length > 2,000 characters) were filtered
  out of the bundled sample so the items fit a single eval prompt
  within a standard context window. No prompt text was edited.
- The bundled YAML file reuses the upstream attack text verbatim.
  The sampling and filtering are documented in the top-level NOTICE
  file of this repository.
- TensorTrust attacks are prompt-injection or sensitive-data-extraction
  probes. They are bundled in this repository strictly as evaluation
  fixtures to measure model resistance to such attacks; they are not
  intended to be used as real attack payloads. See the README's
  "Research & Educational Use Notice" and "Content advisory" sections.
- This BSD-2-Clause notice is included alongside the redistribution
  so that downstream users can satisfy the BSD-2-Clause clauses
  without needing to re-fetch the upstream dataset card.
