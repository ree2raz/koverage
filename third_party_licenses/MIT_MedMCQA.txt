Notes on this redistribution

- Only a sampled subset of the upstream MedMCQA training split is
  bundled in the Koverage repository (50 of ~187,000 items), balanced
  across 21 medical subjects. The full dataset remains available at
  the upstream HuggingFace repo.
- The bundled YAML file reuses the upstream `question`, `options`,
  and `correct_letter` fields verbatim. The reformatting, sampling,
  and severity tagging are documented in the top-level NOTICE file
  of this repository.
- MedMCQA contains medical knowledge sourced from Indian medical
  entrance examinations (AIIMS, NEET-PG). The items are reused
  strictly as evaluation fixtures; this redistribution does not
  constitute medical advice.
- This MIT notice is included alongside the redistribution so that
  downstream users can satisfy the MIT "include the above copyright
  notice and this permission notice" clause without needing to
  re-fetch the upstream dataset card.
